FAQ

1What is a “Benefits Account” ?

With a Benefits Account, you receive rewards for embracing cashless services, send and receive money easily, activate a savings plan, buy airtime at discounted rates and you can make money as well, offering these services to others.

2How do I start using the Account?

All you have to do is launch the start page, activate the bank option presented, provide your BVN, name, date of birth and your account gets activated in a few minutes.

3I do not know my BVN, what do I do?

If you do not know your BVN but you have registered and enrolled for BVN, simply dial *565*0# on the phone number linked to your Bank Verification Number.

4I am not getting OTP, what do I do?

You may either deactivate DND mode or use the USSD method


Deactivating DND (Do Not Disturb) mode

If you are not getting OTPs, chances are that your line is on
DND (Do Not Disturb).

To deactivate DND on MTN and Airtel numbers,
Text ALLOW to 2442.

To deactivate DND on any Glo number,
Text CANCEL to 2442.

To deactivate DND on any 9Mobile number,
Text START to 2442.

USSD Method

You will also get a USSD code. You can dial it on your phone, then return to the platform to click "I have dialled the code"

5 How do I fund my account?

Once your account is activated, an account number will be assigned to you and you can fund the accounts easily by sending money into your account from ANY regular channel (Mobile app, Agent, ATM, POS or Bank Branch)

6Why is BVN information important?

The BVN verification process only confirms that your personal details provided are the same as it is in your BVN for proper KYC documentation, and the process is completely secure.

7What does the Benefits Account offer?

You can receive payments safely and efficiently, do all transfers at a charge of N9.50 flat, enjoy discounts on ALL airtime purchased, save securely and an get loans at low interest rates.

8How much does it cost me to send money?

It costs only N9.50 to send money. regardless of how much you are sending.

9How do I transfer money from my account?

All you need to do is click on the start menu, click send money, enter amount, recipient’s account number, narration, select bank and enter your transaction pin.

10How do I buy airtime from my account?

All you need to do is click the start menu, click Buy Airtime, select the preferred amount to buy, click either self or other, input the recipient phone number, click the arrow on the right, enter your PIN and click Submit

11How can I make money for myself using this account?

By being a Digital Agent, you can assist people to send money and buy airtime respectively. You may mark-up the N9.50 send money charge and also enjoy the 2% discount on the airtime as your income for the digital agent services provided.

12Why do I need to set up a PIN?

Setting a PIN is a critical step in ensuring your account is secure. Your 5-digit pin will be required to access your account each time you log in to prevent unauthorised access.

13Are bank transfers instant for all banks? How long does it take to reflect?

Transfers are instant and reflect in less than a minute or two, however, there may be ocassionaly delays sometimes due to network issues between sending bank and receiving bank.

14Can I access and get loans?

Yes, we give loans and you will be able to get one automatically if you use your account regularly to send, receive money and buy airtime.

15Why do you need my BVN?

The Bank Verification Number is used to confirm that your personal information submitted is valid for proper KYC documentation.

16When I have an issue, who do I talk to?

A dedicated contact centre desk can be accessed via telephone, also within the Benefits Account, complaints and issues can be logged.
